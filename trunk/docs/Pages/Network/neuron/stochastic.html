Stochastic Neuron

This neuron fires randomly with a fixed probability, ignoring input. When a Stochastic Neuron fires, its activation becomes equal to its upper bound, otherwise resting at its lower bound.

Firing Probability

This parameter determines the probability that the neuron will fire, causing it to have an activation equal to its upper bound, given an iteration.
